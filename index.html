Chris Wright is a Newtownabbey-based DJ specialising in Trance, Clubland and Old Skool dance music. After years of watching from the crowd, he got in touch with local legend Craig Dalzell in 2019, started taking lessons, and never looked back — Craig still mentors him today. Since then he's played clubs, festivals, weddings and private parties the length and breadth of Northern Ireland, and built a reputation for reading a room and keeping it moving all night long.
